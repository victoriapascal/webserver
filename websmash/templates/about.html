About gutSMASH

gutSMASH allows the rapid genome-wide identification, annotation and analysis of specialized primary metabolic gene clusters across the anaerobic bacteria with special emphasis from the human gut microbiome. It is a specialized extension of the widely used antiSMASH tool, tailored specifically to target plant genomes.

gutSMASH is powered by several open source tools: NCBI BLAST+, Diamond, HMMer 3, Muscle 3, GlimmerHMM, CD-HIT, PySVG, JQuery SVG, JQuery DataTables, InCHlib, and vis.js.

gutSMASH is the product of a collaborative effort between the Bioinformatics Group of Wageningen University & Research, the Department of Bioengineering and ChEM-H and the Department of Pathology at the Stanford University.
